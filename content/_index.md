WCS Collective is an open, community-built space for people learning West Coast Swing. It exists to help dancers make sense of what they’re learning, practice with intention, and feel more confident between classes and socials.

### Why WCS Collective exists

When you’re new to West Coast Swing, it’s common to feel:

- Confused by terminology used in class
- Unsure what you’re actually supposed to practice
- Lost between lessons and socials
- Afraid you’re “doing it wrong” without realizing it

<--->

### WCS Collective is

A learner-led, community-reviewed project
A place for shared notes, concepts, and questions
A companion resource to support real dancing and real classes
Open, evolving, and built in public

<--->

### WCS Collective is not:

A certification program
An online replacement for teachers or studios
A promise of mastery or shortcuts
A single “correct” way to dance
